02 · Compliance Tech · Real Estate

LayoutIQ — AP Layout Compliance Checker

A compliance checker for Licensed Town Planners preparing layout plans for Andhra Pradesh's DTCP and urban development authorities — parses APDPMS-generated layout plan PDFs, or takes manual plot entry, and validates plot dimensions, road widths, and open-space reservations against the selected authority's rules before submission.

Real-world problem: LTPs are legally accountable for the layout plans they certify, and DTCP/UDA rejections over compliance issues cost weeks of resubmission. LayoutIQ catches violations before submission instead of after.

Covers all AP DTCP + UDA authorities

Real-world problem: Signature-based antivirus struggles to catch new or obfuscated Android malware. This tool classifies network traffic behavior in real time with a trained ML model, catching malicious apps that signature matching misses.

05 · Data Science

Water Quality Prediction System

A machine learning system predicting Ganga River water quality from physicochemical parameters — pH, dissolved oxygen, BOD, turbidity, conductivity — using Scikit-learn classification models, with an 80/20 train-test split evaluated on accuracy, precision, and recall.

Real-world problem: Lab-testing river water for pollution and potability is slow and resource-intensive for monitoring agencies. This system predicts water quality directly from physicochemical readings, enabling faster, cheaper screening.
